# MotusDAO Brand System

## Positioning Core
MotusDAO should be presented as a clinically serious, intellectually modern, and structurally thoughtful brand. For the current funnel, the focus is not broad health-tech futurism. The focus is the transition from clínica presencial to clínica digital with greater ethical, technical, and logical clarity.

## Tone
Use language that feels:
- clinically aware
- clear
- precise
- calm
- rigorous
- modern

Avoid language that feels:
- generic wellness
- motivational fluff
- startup hype
- productivity guru
- overly abstract psychoanalytic jargon for cold audiences

## Messaging Emphasis
Preferred themes:
- clínica digital
- transición clínica
- encuadre
- escucha
- síntoma
- ética
- técnica
- lógica

Do not center these in cold-funnel communication:
- AI as the main promise
- automation
- tools-first positioning
- broad system change narratives unrelated to the course promise

## Visual Direction
Primary direction: cinematic editorial tech-clinical.

Characteristics:
- strong visual hierarchy
- high readability
- premium and serious atmosphere
- dark or controlled contrast backgrounds
- minimal clutter
- one dominant visual idea per slide

Avoid as core identity:
- meme-first systems
- chaotic collage systems
- playful wellness aesthetics

## Carousel Template Logic
Primary template family:
- Cinematic Clinical

Secondary template family:
- Experimental or provocative, used sparingly for tests

## Brand Integrity Rules
- Every asset should feel aligned with the course and funnel promise.
- Clinical seriousness should not become dry abstraction.
- Modern visuals should not erase trust or clarity.
- Educational assets should feel useful, not ornamental.
