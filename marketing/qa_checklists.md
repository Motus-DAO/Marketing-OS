# QA Checklists

## Content QA
- Is the funnel stage explicit?
- Is the message aligned to the course and masterclass?
- Is the hook strong enough?
- Is the CTA appropriate for the funnel stage?
- Is the language clear and on-brand?

## Design QA
- Is the slide hierarchy readable on mobile?
- Is there too much text on any slide?
- Is the visual system consistent with MotusDAO brand rules?
- Is the CTA slide visually distinct enough?
- Does the asset feel premium, serious, and modern?

## Funnel QA
- Does the asset serve the right stage: awareness, education, objection, or conversion?
- Does it move the audience one step forward?
- Is it likely to attract the right type of psychologist?
- Does it avoid irrelevant traffic such as tools-only or productivity-first audiences?

## Publishing QA
- Is the final caption aligned with the asset?
- Is the CTA link or next step clear?
- Has the task status been updated?
- Has the content item been updated in Content Engine?
