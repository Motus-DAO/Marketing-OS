# Funnel Strategy

## Current Objective
Sell the course Psicología Digital through a lower-friction educational funnel that first captures qualified leads with a free masterclass.

## Funnel
Meta Ads -> landing page -> free masterclass -> CRM follow-up -> retargeting -> paid course sale

## Why This Funnel
The course promise is clinically deeper and more specialized than a typical cold-traffic offer. A free masterclass allows the market to be educated first, lowers friction, and improves lead quality before the sales offer.

## Product Positioning
The course should not be sold as a tools course or AI-for-psychologists course. The central promise is the transition to clínica digital with greater ethical, technical, and logical structure.

## Promise
Comprende el cambio de paradigma hacia la clínica digital y transiciona tu práctica con mayor estructura ética, técnica y lógica.

## Target Audience
Primary:
- psychologists
- advanced psychology students
- clinicians already attending online or considering online practice

Secondary:
- therapists
- psychoanalysts
- mental health professionals with interest in digital practice

## Messaging Priorities
Communicate:
- transition in clinical practice
- changes in encuadre and listening
- structural and ethical demands of digital practice
- need for a more refined orientation beyond superficial adaptation

Do not center:
- AI as the primary promise
- productivity tools
- automation
- broad health-tech narratives in cold acquisition

## Lead Magnet
Free masterclass on how clinical practice is changing in the digital era.

## Key Masterclass Outcomes
1. What really changes when moving from presencial to digital clinical practice.
2. How to read and orient practice beyond closed diagnostic thinking.
3. What ethical, technical, and logical structure today’s digital practice requires.

## Operating Priority
Use content and ads to produce qualified registrations first, then use CRM and retargeting to produce course sales.
